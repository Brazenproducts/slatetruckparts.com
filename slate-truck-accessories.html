Best Accessories for the Slate Auto Truck

The Slate Auto EV pickup is built to be customized. Here are the top aftermarket accessories Slate truck owners are buying right now — from day-one protection to serious upgrades.

About the Slate Auto Electric Pickup

The Slate Auto truck is a purpose-built electric pickup truck targeting a starting price of around $20,000 — making it one of the most affordable new trucks ever sold in the United States. Developed with backing from Jeff Bezos among others, Slate Auto's philosophy is radical simplicity: no tablet-sized screens, no unnecessary complexity, no bloated pricing. Just a capable, efficient electric truck that real people can afford.

The Slate truck is expected to be popular with small business owners, tradespeople, urban drivers, and first-time truck buyers. Its minimalist design makes it ideal for customization — which is exactly why aftermarket accessories for the Slate truck are in high demand even before the truck ships.

Getting accessories sorted early is smart. Many Slate truck owners plan to order seat covers, floor mats, and bed protection from day one to keep the truck in top condition from delivery.
